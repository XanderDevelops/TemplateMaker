How does this work?

Provide Us With The Square Footage

Get your home’s square footage. This includes both livable and non-livable spaces (attic, basement, attached garage etc.). Here is our calculation guide to help you find your total: Square Footage Calculator

Select Roof Complexity

Select the roof’s structural complexity level that your architectural plans most closely resemble. Examples for each level are found in the product page images. If you’re still unsure, Contact Us for help.

Opt Into Expedited Delivery

In a pinch and need your plans ASAP? Select expedited delivery before adding package to the cart. We’ll cut the delivery time in half.

Select Additional Services

  • Face-to-Face Meeting or Virtual Consultation
  • Minor Revisions to Architectural CAD Drawings
  • Physical Copy of Structural Plans

Place Your Order

Head over to checkout to review and place your order. If you have any questions throughout the process, contact us. We can set up your cart for you.

Sit Back and Relax

After we receive payment for your order, it will officially be on our schedule. Delivery times vary and are shown on product pages under details. We’ll contact you with any additional info we may need to complete the plans.

We Deliver Your Structural Plans

When your order is finished, you will receive a pdf of your plans. If you made an account with us, you can access the completed plans via the downloads section of your account page.
