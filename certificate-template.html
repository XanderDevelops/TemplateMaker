What this template is for

Certificates often share the same design while only the recipient details change. CSVLink lets you connect certificate text fields to spreadsheet columns and export personalized files in bulk.

Spreadsheet columns you can map

Use these as starter columns, then rename or add fields based on your own workflow.

ColumnHow it is used in the template
Recipient NameThe person receiving the certificate.
ProgramThe course, event, workshop, or program name.
Award TitleThe recognition, achievement, or credential.
Completion DateThe date shown on the certificate.
SignerThe person or role signing the certificate.
OrganizationYour company, school, or team name.

Frequently asked questions

Can I generate certificates from a CSV list?
Yes. Upload the list, map the name and certificate details, preview, and export personalized certificate PDFs.
Can I use my own certificate background?
Yes. You can import a one-page PDF design and place spreadsheet-driven text on top.
Can each certificate have a different name and date?
Yes. Map those fields to spreadsheet columns and CSVLink will use the row data for each output.
